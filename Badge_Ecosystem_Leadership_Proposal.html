The Opportunity

From transactional app to
daily wellness habit.

Badges & Achievements is a behavioural engagement system — not an icon set. It gives every employee a reason to return on the days with no challenge, no event and no appointment.

Engagement is episodic

Wellness apps engage at onboarding and during challenges — then activity decays. Nothing carries motivation between campaigns.

Recognition is winner-only

Leaderboards reward the top 3. The median employee gets nothing — and quietly disengages.

Health needs repetition

One 10K-step day or one health check has limited value. Outcomes come from repeated behaviour — which is exactly what recognition sustains.

Engagement over time — the recognition gap
With badge loop — act · recognise · reward · repeatTypical wellness app
Vision

Make the healthy action the rewarding action — recognise every step of every employee's wellness journey, automatically, and turn recognition into retention.
